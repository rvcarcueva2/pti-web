As we begin the spring semester, PTI is excited to announce the opening of our new campus facilities, featuring modern laboratories and collaborative learning spaces.

#### Facility Highlights

##### Advanced Computer Laboratory
- 50 high-performance workstations
- Latest software and development tools
- Specialized equipment for programming and design

##### Engineering Workshop
- Modern machinery and tools
- Safety-first design
- Hands-on learning opportunities

##### Student Commons
- Collaborative study spaces
- Comfortable seating areas
- High-speed internet throughout

#### Investment in Education

This $2 million investment represents PTI's commitment to providing our students with the best possible learning environment. The new facilities will support both current programs and future expansion plans.

#### Grand Opening Event

Join us for the official grand opening ceremony on February 1st, 2024, at 2:00 PM. The event will include facility tours, demonstrations, and refreshments.

These new facilities will help PTI continue to lead in technical education and prepare our students for successful careers in their chosen fields.
