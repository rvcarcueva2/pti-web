We are proud to announce that Maria Santos, a second-year student in our Advanced Technical Program, has won first place in the National Technical Skills Competition held in Manila last week.

#### Competition Details

The National Technical Skills Competition brought together over 200 students from technical institutes across the Philippines. Participants competed in various categories including:

- Software Development
- Network Administration
- Digital Design
- Engineering Solutions


#### Maria's Achievement

Maria competed in the Software Development category, where she impressed judges with her innovative mobile application that helps connect local farmers with consumers.

> "I'm incredibly grateful for the support and training I've received at PTI. This victory belongs not just to me, but to all the faculty and fellow students who helped me along the way." - Maria Santos

####  Recognition

The competition win comes with a scholarship award and an internship opportunity with one of the Philippines' leading technology companies.

We congratulate Maria on this outstanding achievement and look forward to seeing her continued success!
